HR-Work-Force-Analytics-Report
    An enterprise-level Power BI data solution engineered to deliver actionable intelligence across key human resource dimensions. This report optimizes workforce planning by consolidating disparate organizational data points into dynamic metrics covering headcount distribution, structural trends, diversity, and talent mobility.

Core Analytics & Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The analytical surface area is split into three core tracks designed to assist strategic HR management:

Demographics & Dynamic Headcount:
    -Talent Distribution: Real-time evaluation of personnel counts filtered by regional hubs, cost centers, business units, and functional grading hierarchies.
    -Diversity & Inclusion Indices: High-level reporting metrics looking across organizational tiers to measure equity parameters.
Attrition & Retention Metrics
    -Voluntary vs. Involuntary Churn: Segmented operational ratios separating structured workforce optimization actions from voluntary employee resignations.
    -Risk Modeling Indicators: Correlation tracking between average tenure lengths, localized management changes, performance appraisal histories, and active attrition vectors.
Workforce Cost Optimization
    -FTE Value Extraction: Financial roll-ups benchmarking direct operational headcount spending against standard departmental budget allotments.

Engineering & Technical Details
    -Platform Engine: Power BI Desktop / Power BI Service Premium Runtime
    -Data Model Architecture: Star Schema design consisting of central transaction fact tables linked to optimized, conformed dimension structures.
    -Data Ingestion Pipeline: Power Query (M Language) script transformations parsing raw enterprise database records into structured reporting layers.
